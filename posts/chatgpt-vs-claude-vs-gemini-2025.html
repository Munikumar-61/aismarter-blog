ChatGPT — The All-Rounder

ChatGPT by OpenAI

ChatGPT remains the most widely used AI assistant in the world. Launched in 2022, it set the standard for conversational AI and continues to lead in versatility and third-party integrations.

The GPT-4o model available on the free plan is genuinely impressive — handling everything from coding to creative writing, image generation, and data analysis in one place.

Claude — The Best Writer

Claude by Anthropic

Claude is built by Anthropic with a focus on being helpful, harmless, and honest. It has quickly become the favourite AI assistant among writers, researchers, and professionals who need nuanced, high-quality text.

Claude's writing quality is noticeably better than its competitors — producing text that sounds natural, thoughtful, and human. It handles extremely long documents better than any other AI thanks to its massive 200k token context window.

Gemini — The Google Powerhouse

Gemini by Google

Google's Gemini is the most deeply integrated AI assistant for people who live in the Google ecosystem. It connects directly with Gmail, Google Docs, Google Drive, and Google Search — making it uniquely powerful for office workers.

Gemini Ultra has the largest context window of any AI at 1 million tokens — meaning it can read and analyse entire books, codebases, or long research documents in one go.

Our Final Verdict

ChatGPT Best for most people. If you want one AI that does everything — writing, coding, images, voice — ChatGPT is the safest choice. Its free plan is excellent and the paid plan at $20/month is worth every cent.
Claude Best for writers and bloggers. If you create long-form content, write reports, or analyse documents regularly — Claude's writing quality is in a league of its own. This is the AI we personally use for blog writing.
Gemini Best for Google users. If your work revolves around Gmail, Google Docs, and Google Drive — Gemini's deep integration makes it the most practical choice. Its free plan is also the most generous of the three.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI is completely free to use?
All three — ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini — have free plans. Gemini's free plan is the most generous in 2025, followed closely by ChatGPT. Claude's free plan has stricter usage limits but the quality is exceptional.
Is ChatGPT still the best AI in 2025?
ChatGPT remains the most versatile and widely used AI, but it is no longer the clear winner in every category. Claude beats it for writing quality, and Gemini beats it for Google integration and context window size.
Which AI should a blogger use?
We recommend Claude for writing blog articles — the output quality is noticeably more natural and engaging. Use ChatGPT for brainstorming article ideas and creating images. Use Gemini for research and fact checking.
Can I use all three AI tools together?
Absolutely. Many professionals use all three together — Claude for writing, ChatGPT for coding and images, and Gemini for research. All have free plans so you can use all three at zero cost.
Which AI is most accurate?
Gemini has the edge for real-time accuracy because it connects directly to Google Search. Claude is the most honest about its limitations. ChatGPT can occasionally produce inaccurate information, so always verify important facts.
