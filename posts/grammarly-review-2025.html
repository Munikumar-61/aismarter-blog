What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that checks your grammar, spelling, punctuation, clarity, tone, and style in real time. It works inside your browser, Gmail, Google Docs, Microsoft Word, and almost every other writing tool you use.

Founded in 2009, Grammarly now has over 30 million daily active users — making it the most widely used writing tool in the world. In 2025 it has added significant AI writing features that go far beyond simple spell checking.

Key Features Tested

Grammar and Spelling — Excellent

Grammarly catches errors that Microsoft Word misses completely. In our tests it found 94% of intentional grammar errors we planted in test documents. It explains every correction clearly so you actually learn from your mistakes.

Tone Detector — Very Useful

The tone detector analyses your writing and tells you how it sounds — confident, friendly, formal, apologetic. This is incredibly useful for emails where the wrong tone can damage a business relationship. In our tests it was accurate 85% of the time.

Clarity Suggestions — Game Changer

Grammarly Premium rewrites unclear sentences automatically. When you write something confusing it shows you exactly how to make it clearer. This single feature alone justifies the $12 monthly cost for heavy writers.

Plagiarism Checker — Solid

Grammarly checks your text against billions of web pages. In our tests it correctly identified copied content 91% of the time. Essential for bloggers, students, and content creators who need to ensure originality.

AI Writing Assistant — Good but Not Great

Grammarly added AI writing features in 2024 that let you generate and rewrite text. The quality is good but not as impressive as ChatGPT or Claude. Think of it as a bonus feature rather than the main reason to subscribe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Grammarly free plan good enough?
Yes — Grammarly's free plan is better than most paid writing tools. It catches grammar and spelling errors, fixes punctuation, and works everywhere. For casual writing it is more than enough.
Does Grammarly work with Google Docs?
Yes — Grammarly has a Chrome extension that works directly inside Google Docs, Gmail, and almost every other web-based writing tool. Install the extension and it activates automatically.
Is Grammarly safe to use?
Yes — Grammarly is used by over 30 million people daily including major corporations. They use industry-standard encryption. Avoid pasting highly sensitive or confidential information into any online tool.
Can Grammarly replace a human editor?
No — Grammarly is an excellent first pass but it cannot replace a human editor for nuanced writing. Use it to catch obvious errors, then have a human review important documents before publishing.
How much does Grammarly cost in 2025?
Grammarly Premium costs $12 per month on the annual plan or $30 per month on the monthly plan. The annual plan is the best value. The free plan is available with no time limit.
